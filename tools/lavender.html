Lavender

An email coaching assistant built for outbound sales teams that want clearer, more effective prospect emails.

What it’s for

Use Lavender when your main goal is sales and you want a dedicated sales tool rather than a broad general assistant.

Who should use it

Best for sales team, founder, marketer, and teams with a repeatable workflow they want to speed up.
