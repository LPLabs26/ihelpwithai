Fireflies vs Otter

Two strong meeting-note tools with different strengths for teams, sales, and operations.

The short answer

Choose Fireflies for more workflow-oriented follow-up and team use. Choose Otter if you want a simpler transcript-first experience.

Compare the tools

Meeting workflow Fireflies.ai Free to try

Fireflies

A strong meeting assistant for recording calls, pulling summaries, and keeping follow-ups from slipping.

Best for: Best for sales teams, operators, agencies, and client-facing teams in lots of calls.

Real use case: After every discovery call, create a summary, action list, and CRM-ready handoff without doing it by hand.

Watch-outs: It works best when your team is comfortable with meeting recording, permissions, and review workflows.

Meeting workflow Otter.ai Free to try

Otter

A simple meeting note-taker for transcripts, summaries, and searchable call records.

Best for: Best for teams, students, and operators who want less note-taking overhead.

Real use case: Capture a customer interview, then search the transcript later for objections, requests, and next steps.

Watch-outs: If you want more advanced meeting intelligence or partner monetization paths, Fireflies may be stronger.

Which meeting assistant is the better first pick?

Decision rules

  • Choose Fireflies if the goal is better follow-up, sales handoff, and workflow leverage after meetings.
  • Choose Otter if the main need is fast transcripts and searchable notes with less setup.
  • If monetization matters to the site owner, Fireflies also has a public affiliate path.
