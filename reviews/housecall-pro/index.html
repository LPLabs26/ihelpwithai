Verdict

Housecall Pro earns its place for growing field service teams. The GPS tracking, smart dispatch, and consumer financing integrations are genuinely better than Jobber at scale. The tradeoff is complexity and price — if you have fewer than 5 people, the extra features add setup time without adding proportional value. Start with Jobber if you're under 5 people and revisit HCP when you hit the limits.
