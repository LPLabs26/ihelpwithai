Best AI tools for sales

These tools are worth comparing when you need help with prospecting, outreach, follow-up, and sales enablement work.

What this guide helps with

Use these for outbound messaging, lead handling, and revenue-team workflows.

Best tools to compare first

Meeting workflow Fathom Free to try

Fathom

A very approachable AI meeting assistant for notes, action items, summaries, and cleaner post-call follow-up.

Use it when: Use Fathom when your biggest problem is losing time after meetings and you want the notes, highlights, and next steps handled fast.

Real use case: Turn a customer call into a clean summary, a short follow-up email, and a list of next actions for the owner.

Meeting workflow Fireflies.ai Free to try

Fireflies

A strong meeting assistant for recording calls, pulling summaries, and keeping follow-ups from slipping.

Use it when: Use Fireflies when you want automatic notes, searchable transcripts, and meeting follow-up help without manual admin work.

Real use case: After every discovery call, create a summary, action list, and CRM-ready handoff without doing it by hand.

Video workflow HeyGen Free to try

HeyGen

A strong option for avatar-led videos, translated talking-head content, and business videos that need to look polished fast.

Use it when: Use HeyGen when you want presenter-style videos without filming each version by hand, especially for sales, onboarding, or multilingual content.

Real use case: Create a personalized sales intro or onboarding explainer in multiple languages without re-recording the presenter.

The easiest way to connect apps and let AI actually trigger work, not just answer questions.

Use it when: Use Zapier to move data between apps, create AI-assisted workflows, and automate repetitive business steps.
