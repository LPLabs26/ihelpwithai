Methodology

How we pick and review tools

1

We focus on service businesses

Our readers run field service companies (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing) and beauty & wellness businesses (salons, barbers, medspas, nails, lashes). We only review tools relevant to these niches.

2

We test and research each tool

We read documentation, real customer reviews (Gartner, GeTApp, G2), pricing pages, and changelogs. Where possible, we test the product directly.

3

We disclose affiliate relationships

If we earn a commission from a tool, we say so. Affiliate relationships never change our ratings. We include a "who should look elsewhere" section in every review.
