Privacy

SkillForge is a free tool that turns a tutorial video into an agent skill.

What we collect

When you submit a request, we store the YouTube link you provide and the email address you enter, so we can build your skill and email you the result.

How we use it

Your email is used only to send you the skill you requested (or to tell you if it couldn't be built). We don't sell your email or use it for unrelated marketing.

Storage

Submissions and built skills are stored in our backend. Email addresses are kept private and are not displayed anywhere on the site.
