Runway vs Synthesia

Two video tools with very different jobs: creative generation versus business training and explainers.

The short answer

Choose Runway for creative video experiments and visual concepting. Choose Synthesia for explainers, onboarding, and repeatable business videos.

Runway

A strong choice for AI video generation, concept scenes, and fast creative experiments.

Best for: Best for creators and marketers producing ads, teasers, music visuals, or storyboards.

Real use case: Turn a product concept into a short teaser before paying for a full production shoot.

Watch-outs: AI video gets expensive and messy without a clear brief. You still need creative direction and taste.

Video workflow Synthesia Paid

Synthesia

A business-first video tool for training, onboarding, explainers, and internal communication with AI avatars.

Best for: Best for L&D teams, customer support, HR, and businesses that need repeatable instructional content.

Real use case: Create a new employee onboarding video in multiple languages without organizing a shoot.

Watch-outs: If brand personality or realism matters more than speed, recorded video may still feel stronger.

Which video tool should you choose first?

Decision rules

  • Choose Runway if the output needs to feel more cinematic or experimental.
  • Choose Synthesia if the real goal is repeatable instructional or business communication content.
  • If your team records real people regularly, Riverside may still be the better comparison.
