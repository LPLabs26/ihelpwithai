Perplexity

Perplexity

One of the easiest tools for fast research because it answers questions with cited web sources.

What it’s for

Use Perplexity when you need current information, quick market scans, or a research starting point with links to sources.

Who should use it

Best for people who regularly ask, 'Can you find me the latest on this?'

One real use case

Research competitors in a niche and collect source-backed notes before writing a strategy memo.

Why it made the directory

It saves time by combining search, summarizing, and citations in one step.

Watch-outs

It is best as a research front end. You may still want another tool to turn the findings into polished deliverables.

Good first prompt

Research the top 5 competitors in this niche and give me a table with pricing, positioning, and source links.

Prompt maker

Start with the tool's best first prompt, then make it specific to your actual job, audience, and output.
