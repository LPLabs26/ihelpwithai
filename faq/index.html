Is this site free?

Completely. The reviews, comparisons, and shortlist quiz are all free to use. We make money through affiliate commissions if you sign up for a tool through our links. The only thing we charge for is the $97 personal written recommendation.

How do you pick which tools to review?

We focus on tools that service businesses actually use — field service management, booking, review generation, and call tracking. We prioritize tools with affiliate programs because that's how we stay free, but we only recommend tools we believe are actually good.

What is the $97 recommendation?

Yuobuy a personal written recommendation. You fill out a form, we read it, and we send you a written report within 1-2 business days. It covers what we think fits your situation, what to watch out for, and if anything we would not use at your stage.

Are your reviews biased?

We try hard not to be. We always disclose affiliate relationships. We always include a "who should look elsewhere" section. We don't accept payment for positive reviews.
