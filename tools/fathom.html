Fathom

A very approachable AI meeting assistant for notes, action items, summaries, and cleaner post-call follow-up.

What this tool does

Use Fathom when your biggest problem is losing time after meetings and you want the notes, highlights, and next steps handled fast.

Who this is a good fit for

Best for founders, account teams, operators, and anyone who wants meeting follow-up without a lot of setup.

A good first use case

Turn a customer call into a clean summary, a short follow-up email, and a list of next actions for the owner.

Why you might pick it

It is one of the easiest meeting-note tools to start using well on day one.

Skip it if

If you need deeper conversation intelligence, coaching, or a larger system around calls, a heavier platform may fit better.

What to try first

Summarize this call into the top decisions, risks, next actions, and one short follow-up email I can send today.
