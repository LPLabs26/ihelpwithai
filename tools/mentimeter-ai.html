Mentimeter AI

An interactive presentation tool with AI help for creating audience-engaging sessions and live polls.

What it’s for

Use Mentimeter AI when your main goal is presentations and you want a dedicated presentations tool rather than a broad general assistant.

Who should use it

Best for educator, team, founder, and teams with a repeatable workflow they want to speed up.

One real use case

Build an interactive workshop deck with questions, polls, and recap slides.

Why it made the directory

It earns a place because it can make presentations work faster and more practical for people who want a clearer presentations workflow.

Watch-outs

It is strongest for faster first drafts. You may still refine the final deck manually.
