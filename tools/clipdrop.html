Clipdrop

A quick image editing toolkit for cleanup, object removal, background work, and generative tweaks.

What it’s for

Use Clipdrop when your main goal is design and you want a dedicated image tool rather than a broad general assistant.

Who should use it

Best for designer, marketer, creator, and teams with a repeatable workflow they want to speed up.
