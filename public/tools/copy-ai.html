A marketing and sales-focused AI tool for messaging, outreach ideas, and repetitive go-to-market work.

What it’s for

Use Copy.ai when your team is producing lots of outbound copy, campaign ideas, or sales enablement material.

Who should use it

Best for founders, marketers, and sales teams trying to ship messaging faster.
