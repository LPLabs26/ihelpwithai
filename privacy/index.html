Privacy Policy

What data this site collects, how it's used, and how to request deletion. Written in plain language.

What data is collected

ihelpwithai.com collects the following data:

  • Basic analytics — pages visited, referral source, browser type, approximate location (city/country). No personally identifiable information is collected in analytics.
  • Email address — only if you voluntarily submit it via a contact form, intake form, or newsletter signup. Email addresses are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing.
  • Intake form responses — if you purchase the personal recommendation service, your intake form answers are used solely to produce your recommendation and stored securely. They are not shared with vendors or third parties.
  • Payment data — PayPal processes payments. ihelpwithai.com does not store credit card numbers or payment credentials. PayPal's privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

How data is used

  • Analytics data is used to understand which content is useful and improve the site.
  • Email addresses are used to send the intake form, deliver recommendations, or send newsletter issues if subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Intake form responses are used only to write your personal recommendation.

Cookies and third-party tools

This site may use cookies for analytics purposes. Affiliate links use tracking cookies provided by vendor affiliate programs — these cookies allow vendors to attribute referrals to this site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, which will not affect your ability to use this site but may affect affiliate attribution.

Third-party services that may set cookies or collect data include: Google Analytics (analytics), PayPal (payments), and vendor affiliate platforms.

Data retention and deletion

Email addresses are retained for as long as an active communication relationship exists. Intake form responses are retained for up to 12 months after delivery of the recommendation, then deleted.

To request deletion of any data associated with your email address, contact info@ihelpwithai.com. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the site adds new features or data practices. The last-updated date at the bottom of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about this policy: info@ihelpwithai.com
