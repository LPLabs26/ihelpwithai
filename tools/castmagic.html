Castmagic

An AI content repurposing app that turns podcast episodes, meetings, interviews, and recordings into usable written content faster.

What it’s for

Use Castmagic when you want one recording to become summaries, clips, outlines, social copy, and other publishing assets with less manual rewriting.

Who should use it

Best for creators, marketers, and teams that already record useful conversations but struggle to turn them into enough follow-on content.

One real use case

Take a podcast or webinar recording and spin out a summary, show notes, promo copy, and multiple follow-up content ideas in one pass.

Why it made the directory

It is helpful when content extraction and repurposing are the bottleneck rather than generating ideas from a blank page.

Watch-outs

You still need editing judgment for tone, factual accuracy, and what content is actually worth publishing.

Good first prompt

Turn this recording into a clean summary, 5 social posts, 3 newsletter angles, and a short article outline.
