NotebookLM

A source-grounded research notebook for summarizing, organizing, and talking to your documents.

What it’s for

Use NotebookLM when you need AI help based on your own notes, PDFs, transcripts, and research sources.

Who should use it

Best for researchers, analysts, students, and teams working from large document sets.

One real use case

Upload reports, meeting notes, and docs, then ask NotebookLM for a summary and key risks.

Why it made the directory

It keeps the AI anchored to your source material instead of only relying on general web knowledge.

Watch-outs

It is strongest for source synthesis, not full workflow automation or media creation.
