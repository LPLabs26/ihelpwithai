Ready-to-use AI prompts for ChatGPT or Claude. Copy, paste, and adapt to your business. No setup required.

Review request text

Send this after a completed job to get more Google reviews.

Hi [Customer name], thanks for having us out today. If you have 2 minutes, a Google review would mean a lot to us — it helps other homeowners find us when they need help. [Your Google review link] — [Your name], [Business name]
No-answer voicemail script

Leave this when a lead doesn't pick up.

Hi, this is [Your name] from [Business name]. I'm calling about [the estimate you requested / your service call today]. Give me a call back at [number] and we'll get you taken care of. Thanks.
Job complete follow-up text

Send after every completed job.

Hi [Name], just wanted to confirm everything looks good with your [service] today. If you have any questions in the next few days, call or text [number] and we'll take care of it. — [Business name]
Estimate follow-up (3 days later)

Send when a prospect has gone quiet after an estimate.

Hi [Name], following up on the estimate we sent over. Happy to answer any questions or adjust anything before you decide. What works best — a quick call or just text? — [Your name]
ChatGPT prompt: write an SOP

Use this to document any recurring job process.

Write a step-by-step standard operating procedure for [describe the task — e.g., "winterizing an outdoor irrigation system"]. Format it as a numbered checklist a new technician could follow on their first day. Use plain language.
ChatGPT prompt: respond to a negative review

Paste a bad review and get a professional response draft.

A customer left this Google review: "[paste the review]". Write a professional, calm response that acknowledges their concern, takes responsibility where appropriate, and invites them to contact us directly to resolve it. Keep it under 80 words.
