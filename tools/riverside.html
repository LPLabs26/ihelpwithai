Riverside

A strong choice for remote interviews, podcasts, webinars, and creator-friendly recording workflows.

What it’s for

Use Riverside when you need cleaner remote recordings and want AI help turning long content into publishable clips.

Who should use it

Best for podcast hosts, creators, educators, and agencies publishing frequent interview content.

One real use case

Record a remote guest interview, then cut clips, export captions, and publish the best moments quickly.

Why it made the directory

It brings recording quality and repurposing closer together in one workflow.

Watch-outs

If your main job is generating video from scratch instead of recording real people, Runway or Synthesia may fit better.

Good first prompt

Find the 5 strongest clip moments in this interview and give each one a headline and caption idea.
