Scholarcy

A paper summarization tool for extracting key points, flashcards, and digestible takeaways from dense material.

What it’s for

Use Scholarcy when your main goal is research and you want a dedicated research tool rather than a broad general assistant.

Who should use it

Best for student, educator, analyst, and teams with a repeatable workflow they want to speed up.

One real use case

Turn a long research paper into a faster executive digest before a meeting or literature review.

Why it made the directory

It earns a place because it can make research work faster and more practical for people who want a clearer research workflow.

Watch-outs

It helps most with finding and structuring information. You may still want another tool for final deliverables.

Good first prompt

Help me do this job well: Turn a long research paper into a faster executive digest before a meeting or literature review. Start by outlining the smartest approach, what inputs you need, and a practical first draft or plan.
