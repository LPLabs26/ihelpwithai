How submissions are reviewed.

The goal is not to list everything. The goal is to surface tools that make real buying decisions easier.

1. Editorial fit check

We look for a clear workflow, practical value, and a believable fit for small teams, founders, or operators.

2. Trust and positioning check

We care about plain-English positioning, pricing clarity, setup reality, and whether the tool belongs in a buyer guide.

3. Coverage decision

If it fits, it may appear in the directory first, then move into reviews, comparisons, or guides as confidence grows.
