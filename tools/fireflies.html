Fireflies

A strong meeting assistant for recording calls, pulling summaries, and keeping follow-ups from slipping.

What it’s for

Use Fireflies when you want automatic notes, searchable transcripts, and meeting follow-up help without manual admin work.

Who should use it

Best for sales teams, operators, agencies, and client-facing teams in lots of calls.

One real use case

After every discovery call, create a summary, action list, and CRM-ready handoff without doing it by hand.

Why it made the directory

It solves a real pain fast: people forget details, and meetings rarely turn into clean follow-up by themselves.

Watch-outs

It works best when your team is comfortable with meeting recording, permissions, and review workflows.

Good first prompt

Summarize this meeting into decisions, risks, action items, owners, and next steps I can send to the team.
