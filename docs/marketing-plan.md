# ihelpwithai.com marketing plan

## Main goal
Grow ihelpwithai.com by turning it into a useful AI app discovery site that:
- ranks for high-intent search
- gets shared by app founders
- builds repeat traffic through updates and reviews

## Best channels first
1. SEO for high-intent pages
2. Founder outreach to apps already listed
3. Submit-an-app loop
4. Weekly review/roundup posting
5. Reddit, X, and LinkedIn distribution
6. AI newsletter and directory submissions

## Immediate priorities

### 1) Build the founder loop
- Add a submit-an-app page
- Add submit CTAs on the homepage and footer
- Later add a claim-this-listing CTA on app pages

### 2) Start founder outreach
Contact founders of listed apps with:
- their listing link
- an offer to fix/update details
- a request to share if the page looks right
- a simple badge/link-back option

### 3) Publish repeatable content
Focus on pages and posts like:
- Best AI apps for marketers
- Best free AI apps
- Best AI apps for research
- ChatGPT alternatives
- Claude vs Gemini vs ChatGPT
- New AI apps this week

### 4) Distribute every useful page
For each new roundup or review:
- post on X
- post on LinkedIn
- share selectively on Reddit where relevant
- pitch to AI newsletters and roundups

## 30-day plan

### Week 1
- launch submit-an-app flow
- email or DM founders of the top 50 listed apps
- publish 3 roundup pages from existing content
- start a daily social posting streak

### Week 2
- publish 5 more high-intent pages
- submit the site to launch/discovery platforms
- start collecting app update requests from founders

### Week 3
- publish a weekly “new AI apps” post
- pitch newsletters and curated AI roundups
- create a simple founder badge for listed apps

### Week 4
- review which pages get traction
- push more pages in the winning categories
- improve internal links between directory, reviews, and comparisons

## What success looks like
- more founder shares and backlinks
- more long-tail search traffic
- more app submissions
- more review/update requests
- stronger repeat visibility each week

- claim this listing CTA on app pages
- founder badge/link-back asset
- weekly roundup template
- better email capture for repeat traffic
