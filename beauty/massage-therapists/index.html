Recommended stack by business stage

If the calendar still depends on manual reminders

If visits are great but retention is weak

Use rebooking nudges, wellness cadence reminders, and birthday or loyalty messages.

Use client note summaries, aftercare templates, and simple automations after the core booking system is stable.
